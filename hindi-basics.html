Greetings

In this lesson, you'll learn the most common Hindi greetings used in daily conversations. Greetings are the first step to making connections with Hindi speakers.

Basic Greetings

Namaste

Hello / Greetings

Pronunciation: nuh-muh-stay

Namaskar

Hello (more formal)

Pronunciation: nuh-muh-skar

Shubh prabhaat

Good morning

Pronunciation: shubh pruh-bhaat

Shubh raatri

Good night

Pronunciation: shubh raa-tree

Common Responses

Aap kaise hain?

How are you? (formal)

Pronunciation: aap kai-say hain

Main theek hoon

I'm fine

Pronunciation: main theek hoon

Dhanyavaad

Thank you

Pronunciation: dhun-yuh-vaad

Phir milenge

See you again

Pronunciation: phir mi-len-gay

Cultural Note

In India, people often greet each other with "Namaste" while pressing their palms together near the chest (called "Namaste" gesture). This is considered respectful, especially when greeting elders.

Lesson 2 of 12

Introducing Yourself

In this lesson, you'll learn how to introduce yourself in Hindi, including sharing your name, where you're from, and basic personal information.

Basic Introductions

Mera naam ___ hai

My name is ___

Pronunciation: may-ra naam ___ hai

Main ___ se hoon

I am from ___

Pronunciation: main ___ say hoon

Main ___ saal ka hoon

I am ___ years old

Pronunciation: main ___ saal ka hoon

Mera pesha ___ hai

My profession is ___

Pronunciation: may-ra pay-sha ___ hai

Sample Conversation

Namaste! Aapka naam kya hai? (Hello! What is your name?)

Mera naam Rohan hai. (My name is Rohan)

Aap kahan se hain? (Where are you from?)

Cultural Note

In Indian culture, it's common to ask about someone's profession or family early in a conversation. This is considered polite and shows interest in the other person.

Shopping Phrases

कितना है?
(Kitna hai?)
How much is it?
क्या आप क्रेडिट कार्ड स्वीकार करते हैं?
(Kya aap credit card sweekar karte hain?)
Do you accept credit cards?
क्या आप इसे पैक कर देंगे?
(Kya aap ise pack kar denge?)

क्या आपके पास छूट है?
(Kya aapke paas chhoot hai?)

Practice Exercise
