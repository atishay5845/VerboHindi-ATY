Asking for Items

Learn how to ask for items in a shop.

Common Phrases

क्या आपके पास यह है?

Kya aapke paas yah hai?

Do you have this?

मुझे यह चाहिए

Mujhe yah chaahiye

I need this

Lesson 3 of 8

Price Negotiation

Learn how to negotiate prices in Hindi.

Negotiation Phrases

क्या आप कम कर सकते हैं?

Kya aap kam kar sakte hain?

Can you reduce the price?

यह बहुत महंगा है

Yah bahut mehenga hai

This is too expensive

Lesson 4 of 8

Payment Methods

Learn how to discuss payment methods in Hindi.

Payment Phrases

क्या आप कार्ड स्वीकार करते हैं?

Kya aap card sweekar karte hain?

Do you accept cards?

क्या आपके पास छुट्टा है?

Kya aapke paas chhutta hai?

Do you have change?

Lesson 5 of 8

Returns and Exchanges

Learn how to handle returns and exchanges in Hindi.

Return Phrases

मैं इसे वापस करना चाहता हूं

Main ise vaapas karna chahta hoon

I want to return this

क्या मैं इसे बदल सकता हूं?

Kya main ise badal sakta hoon?

Can I exchange this?

Lesson 6 of 8

Shopping Etiquette

Learn about shopping customs and etiquette in India.

Cultural Notes

  • Bargaining is common in local markets
  • It's polite to greet shopkeepers with "Namaste"
  • Many shops close for lunch between 1-3 PM
  • Sunday is often a busy shopping day
Lesson 7 of 8

Shopping Preferences

Learn how to express your shopping preferences in Hindi.

Preference Phrases

मुझे यह रंग पसंद है

Mujhe yah rang pasand hai

I like this color

क्या आपके पास और विकल्प हैं?

Kya aapke paas aur vikalp hain?

Do you have other options?

Review & Practice

Practice all the phrases you've learned in a simulated shopping conversation.

Practice Exercise

Try this conversation:

Shopkeeper: नमस्ते, आपको क्या चाहिए?

(Hello, what do you need?)

(I want this shirt)

(This is ₹500)
