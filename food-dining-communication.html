Basic Food Vocabulary

In this lesson, you'll learn essential Hindi vocabulary for common foods and drinks.

Common Foods

रोटी

Roti (Bread)

Pronunciation: ro-tee

चावल

Chawal (Rice)

Pronunciation: cha-wal

दाल

Dal (Lentils)

Pronunciation: daal

सब्जी

Sabzi (Vegetables)

Pronunciation: sub-zee

Common Drinks

पानी

Paani (Water)

Pronunciation: paa-nee

चाय

Chai (Tea)

Pronunciation: chai

Cultural Note

In Indian culture, it's common to share food from a common plate. This is considered a sign of closeness and trust. Also, many Indians eat with their right hand, as the left hand is traditionally considered unclean.

Ordering Food

Learn how to order food in Hindi restaurants and cafes.

Basic Ordering Phrases

मैं यह ऑर्डर करना चाहूंगा

Main yah order karna chaahunga

I would like to order this

क्या आप मुझे मेनू दिखा सकते हैं?

Kya aap mujhe menu dikha sakte hain?

Can you show me the menu?

Special Requests

Paying the Bill

Learn how to ask for and pay the bill in Hindi.

Bill Related Phrases

बिल ले आइए

Bill le aaiye

Please bring the bill

Lesson 6 of 8

Restaurant Etiquette

Learn about Indian dining etiquette and customs.

Cultural Notes

  • It's common to share food from the same plate in India
  • Eating with your right hand is traditional
  • It's polite to wait for everyone to be served before starting to eat
  • Leaving a small amount of food on your plate is considered polite
